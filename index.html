How to Test

MatchaTone reacts to your sound and touch.
Try the following actions:

⭐ Sound

  • Soft sound → the character leans in to listen
  • Loud sound → the character becomes shy
  • Quiet for 10 seconds → the character falls asleep

⭐ Touch

  • Tap the character → triggers happy (with music)

⭐ States

  • Live – normal idle
  • Listening – approaches you and listens closely
  • Happy – reacts to your tap with a joyful animation and BGM
  • Shy – startled by loud sound
  • Sleep – falls asleep after long silence, with its own sleep BGM

About MatchaTone

MatchaTone is a gentle companion that listens, reacts, and rests. It breathes with sound, blushes when surprised, and smiles when touched. A small window into emotion through simple interaction.
